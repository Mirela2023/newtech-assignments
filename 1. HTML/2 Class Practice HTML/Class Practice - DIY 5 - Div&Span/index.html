If you are free-spirited and love natural fabrics and color, then bohemian decor could be the right style for your home!
There are lots of ways to bring in bohemian style decor to your space. Bohemian decor is often associated with people in the arts.
It is eclectic by nature and there are no rules when it comes to boho style decor.
Boho decor takes the “more is more” approach, mixing all sorts of colors, patterns, textures, and embellishments whenever desired.
